Designing long-term solutions to tough challenges.

Kalt is a think tank focused on creating local change, with an international perspective. Promoting local autonomy means empowering local communities to be self-starters, giving them stewardship over their environment, and providing support instead of exerting control. We operate on a set of politically agnostic values.
