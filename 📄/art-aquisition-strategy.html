Our goal is to aquire art of exceptional quality from upencoming artists and show them to the local-global community.

Factors

The distinguishing factors between bad/good contemporary art and great contemporary art.

  • The art must have unique characteristics.
  • The artist has an interesting technique to how they produce and/or present the art.
  • The subject matter cannot be blasé.
  • It has to be both currently relevant as well as future relevant, or historically relevant.
  • The medium has to make sense, in some form or another.
