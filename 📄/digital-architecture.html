Digital architecture is no different than software architecture, but the scope is much broader. The goal is to make systems and processes that empower entire organizations to quickly create, iterate, and evolve. This requires the technological solutions to be soft, maluable and diverse. The marketing department should never be limited by the CMS the company chose. With good design systems, software architecture and serverless infrastructure, we can empower the business to do what they do best.

The future will inevitably be something along the lines of static interfaces, with dynamic functionlity, integrated with specilized SaaS solutions, thirdparty APIs all powered by an eventdriven serverless microservice architecture.
