We run a fund that invest in interesting ideas. It could be called alternative fundemental value investing, but that would be too technical. We base them on a set of thesis' and values.

Crypto

The crypto fund aims to be an index fund covering the top 100 crypto. We only invest if they have had an average market cap above 550 million euro over the past 12 months. And are not a stablecoin or a scam.

Artworks

Art is one of the few platforms that give voice to minorities, however, owning and profiting from art has long been exclusive to the super-wealthy. See the artworks.

Private companies

When the right company is raising capital we might consider investing in them. Currently we are only invested in Tillit, a Norwegian insurance company.

Public companies

We rarely invest in public companies, but we have positions in Twitter, Reddit, Airbnb, and Discord.
