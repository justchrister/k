Prioritizing tasks

Dont answer emails, slack messages or teams right away.

In this modern society we have gotten used to being available to everyone all the time. In doing so, we loose focus constantly, instead of focusing on the tasks we should be doing, we often end up communicating with people instead of actually getting things done.

Using the Eisenhower matrix in combination with a goal-oriented list, we delegate or eliminate everything that is not a part of our DO list (5 items).
