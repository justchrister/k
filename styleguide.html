The goal of the visual profile of Kalt is to communicate ideas as clearly as possible, whilst also beeing extremely versatile. This is achived in combination with the Kalt — Stylebook.

The logo

The logotype is set in Helvetica Regular; Kalt. It is always be capitalized, even in URL's.

The logomark — also called the insignia — is the outline of one circle, inside the outline of an oval, inside the outline of another oval. The outlines has to be 1 pixel on digital platforms, and as thin as possible in print, whilst still being visable. While the insignia is a set of one inner circle, and two outer ovals.
