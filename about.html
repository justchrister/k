About

This is a think tank — that is practical, rather than theoretical — with the goal of making everywhere more inclusive and interesting. Through local change, with a global perspective.

Events/exhibtions Our goal is to find interesting and one of a kind curators that can put together unique experiences with up and coming artists.

Art Artists are one of the few that are able to bring up taboo topic, set the agenda for public discussion, and amplify important voices.

Capital Our enourmous goals are capital intensive, and require us to earn money continously. However, profits is a means to an end, and an outcome of doing extraordinary work.

Nomadic Our team is located everywhere, and we have no headquarters or physical location. This lets us find the perfect venue for an event, and work with artists from anywhere.
